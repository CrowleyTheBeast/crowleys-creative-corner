Quest Log #1 — Foundation  May 2026

Entry One. The campfire is just getting started.

Alright — Quest Log number one. If you're reading this, you found the Corner early, and I appreciate that more than you know. I'd say I'm not nervous about anyone actually reading this, but I'd be lying, and the Quest Log is supposed to be honest.

May 2026. The site is up. The pages exist. And I'm sitting here realizing that "the foundation is being built" is the most accurate thing I've ever written about myself.

The big project right now is my book — a High-Fantasy story that I've been building in my head, in my notes, in scattered files, and finally, properly, in an actual system. I have characters with full sheets. A world with six cities, layers, a magic system built on resonance and emotional truth. A main arc — the Corvin/Cult Arc — that I'm genuinely excited about. Themes about family, loyalty, what people do when the price is right, and what it costs to finally look at where you came from.

The honest progress report? The bones are solid. The architecture is there. Actual scenes? That's what Quest Two is going to be about. (My anxiety said to apologize for that. I told it to sit down.)

This entry is mostly just: I started. The Corner exists. The log is open. Which sounds small until you realize how many times I almost talked myself out of it on the walk over. (Anxiety loves a good "maybe just don't" moment. Very classic of it.)

Some quests start with a battle. Some start with sitting down at the table, lighting a candle, and picking up the journal. This one started here.

See you at the next campfire. 🕯️
