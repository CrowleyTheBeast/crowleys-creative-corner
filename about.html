About Me

Nikita Jay Crowley — Storyteller. Worldbuilder. Gamer. Community builder. Work in progress.

This is a tough one. I drafted a few versions trying to figure out what might look right — but I also want my honest voice here, so here we are. (Writing a whole "About Me" page is somehow one of the scariest parts of this. We're already off to a great start.)

I started this because of a habit I had as a kid. Walking to a buddy's house — or really anywhere — I'd find a rock, a can, some random object, and bring it along for the journey. I'd create a little story around it. Or sometimes I just didn't want to be alone, and wanted to enjoy the natural silence for a while. That's kind of where all of this comes from.

This website and everything attached to it has been in the forge for a while. I just finally sat down and started — again — but with a better approach this time. I fail. I make mistakes. There's no such thing as perfect, and too many people have exhausted themselves trying to prove otherwise. I use all sorts of tools to build this, and as history has shown, when you're honest about your tools, you can use them properly.

I do this to show that even with all the options in the world, you can still go your own way. I want to help and support as many people as I can while genuinely enjoying the journey we're all on. I'm also a realist. I know I have privileges others don't — which is where I've learned to be grateful, and to look in the bowl and make sure there's enough. We're all in that position from one perspective or another.

I can feel myself sliding into a philosophy spiral and I apologize. (Anxiety and I have had several meetings about this page. It keeps suggesting I just write "I like things" and call it done.) But being candid and raw feels important here, so you'll get that from me.

I'll try not to spoil things, but I can't stop people who peek further ahead — so please, if you do, don't ruin it for others. There's a summary of what I'm trying to achieve below. So I don't annoy you away if you're still here.

Thank you, and enjoy.

What I'm Building

I'm a storyteller, worldbuilder, gamer, creative thinker, and community builder. I believe creativity can help people feel more connected, more encouraged, and a little less alone in whatever they're carrying.

Crowley's Creative Corner is my home base — the place where stories, games, worldbuilding projects, ideas, and life quests live and grow, one step at a time. My biggest current project is a fantasy novel years in the making: a story about family wounds, chosen loyalty, moral grey areas, and what it really means to be wanted versus loved. It's the kind of story I needed to read, so I decided to write it.

My vision for this Corner is bigger than one book. Over time, I want this to be a real space — for creators, players, dreamers, and people figuring things out. A place for collaborative projects, honest conversations, creative encouragement, and community that actually feels like community.

None of this happens in a vacuum. I'm building from a small space, with real limitations, and I won't pretend otherwise — but I have people in my corner who matter. Family and friends who show up with encouragement when I need it most, and anyone who's taken the time to give me honest, constructive feedback along the way. That kind of support means more than I can properly say, and it's part of why I want this Corner to be a place that gives that back to others.

If you're here because you believe in what's being built — thank you. If you want to support the journey, there are ways to do so. And if you're just here to breathe for a while and see what's happening — welcome. There's always room at the table.
