Support the Corner

This Corner is being built one step at a time — stories, worlds, games, community, and honest creative work. If any of it has meant something to you, there are ways to help it keep going. No pressure, no guilt. Just genuine appreciation for anyone who shows up.

Free Ways to Support

These cost nothing and mean more than you'd think:

  • 🌟 Share the Corner — Send the site to someone who might enjoy it. Word of mouth is how small creative spaces actually grow.
  • 📖 Read the Quest Log — Showing up and following the journey is its own kind of support.
  • 💬 Give honest feedback — On the stories, the site, the ideas. Constructive and kind goes a long way.
  • 📣 Follow on socials — When those spaces go live, being there early helps more than it seems.
  • 🛡️ Encourage the community — Be kind in the spaces here. That's the whole foundation.
Seriously — reading this page already counts. Thank you for being here.

Support on Ko-fi

If you enjoy what's being built here and want to help it grow, Ko-fi is the place. Every bit of support goes directly toward keeping the Corner running and moving forward — website costs, creative tools, project development, and building something worth coming back to.

No tiers, no obligations. Just a way to say "I believe in this" if you feel like it.
