Dimensional Mage

Dimensional Mage is a fantasy story about trauma, growth, found family, strange powers, and the rise of a future Guildmaster.

The story follows Asheron as he learns to face his past, understand his power, build a guild, and become the kind of person who can help others find their way too.

This page will eventually hold story updates, worldbuilding notes, character profiles, chapter previews, and behind-the-scenes development.
