Dimensional Mage

High Fantasy · Novel in Development

A High-Fantasy saga about a trauma-shaped man who learns to turn his wounds into something the world actually needs.

The story follows Asheron — a man with unstable, dangerous magic born from a childhood he didn't ask for, relationships that cost him, and a darkness he spends most of his life trying to outrun. It's about found family, hard-earned healing, dimensional chaos, and what it means to become someone other people can actually rely on.

The question at the heart of it all: can someone who has caused harm, suffered harm, and carries real darkness still become a source of light? Asheron spends the entire saga finding out. So does the reader.

This isn't a story about a chosen hero who was always going to save the world. It's about someone who had to choose — over and over, badly sometimes — before he became the person the world needed. The Guildmaster. The World-Sealer. The man who learned that broken people aren't doomed to stay broken.

It's been living in my head for a long time. It's finally getting out.
