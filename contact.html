What, you want to contact me?

Bold move. I respect it. Whether you've got feedback, a collab idea, a question about the book, or just want to say hey — the door's open. This is the place.

What can you reach out about?

Genuinely, most things. But here's what lands in my inbox well:

  • Feedback — Honest, constructive thoughts on anything at the Corner. The site, the writing, the ideas. I actually read it.
  • Collabs — Got a creative project, community idea, or something you think we could build together? Let's talk.
  • Community questions — Questions about the Corner, the Quest Board, what's coming — send them over.
  • Book interest — Curious about Dimensional Mage? Want updates, early looks, or just to nerd out about the world? Welcome.
  • Anything else — Seriously. If it felt worth sending, it probably was.
One thing I'll ask: be kind. Not perfect — kind. That's all. (Anxiety is already in the passenger seat when I open my inbox. Don't give it a reason to drive.)
